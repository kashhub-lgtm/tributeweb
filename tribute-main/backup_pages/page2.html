Page 2 • Guru Vandana

To My Respected Mentor

🪔

गुरुर्ब्रह्मा गुरुर्विष्णुः
गुरुर्देवो महेश्वरः।

गुरुः साक्षात् परब्रह्म
तस्मै श्री गुरवे नमः॥

"A Guru is the creator, the protector, and the guide. My heartfelt respect to the teacher who lights the path of knowledge."

Peace. Gratitude. Blessings.
